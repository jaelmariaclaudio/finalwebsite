VISION

S.Sp.S Educational Institutions afire with the spirit of excellence

GOALS

To provide relevant and quality education rooted in Christian Values
To practice “Truth in Love” in our relationships
To strengthen vocation animation in the school programs
To intensify care for Mother Earth
To intensify outreach
To encourage volunteerism
To promote responsible use of technology
To form competent leaders responsive to local, national and global issues
To build communities that promotes peace, justice, unity and respect for life
To reach out to people who live in poverty of any kind
To exercise more participative/ team leadership at all levels
To strengthen our networking with the other ministries of the Rosary Province
To invest in the formation of our lay partners and allow them to take responsible positions

MISSION

Guided by the charism of St. Arnold Janssen and the Trinitarian Mission Spirituality, we build through Christian and Holistic Formation, new generations of responsible citizens who are agents of change in society.
